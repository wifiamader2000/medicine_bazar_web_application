How do I order medicines?

Browse our shop, add medicines to cart, proceed to checkout, and select your payment method.

Some medicines require a prescription. These are marked with "Rx" badge.

We accept Cash on Delivery, Nagad, bKash, Upay, and Merchant payments.

Yes, within 7 days for damaged or wrong products. See our Return Policy.

How do I upload a prescription?

Go to "Upload Prescription" page, fill in the details, and upload the image/PDF.

Is my prescription data secure?

Yes, prescription files are stored securely and only accessible by authorized pharmacists.
